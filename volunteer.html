Become a Volunteer!


Got a passion for helping people out. Be apart of our students’ exceptional experience.

Whether you have one hour to contribute occasionally, or have a desire to be a weekly reading buddy, every opportunity to work with you is valued and appreciated.


Each year volunteers help support our students and our school by making impactful contributions like these:

  • Speak to students about their work interests
  • Becoming an active advocate for our school
  • Work with students as reading/ lunch buddies
  • Helping pick up or organize supplies
  • Lead a school campus clean-up projects
  • Mentor a student with similar interests
  • Teach the important of character and integrity
  • Plant flowers in our community garden
  • Help paint and renovate classrooms
  • And more ..
