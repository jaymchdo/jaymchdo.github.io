About the Matrix Parent Center

we believe that parents are our most essential partners, playing an important role in the success of our students.

Static and dynamic content editing

Our goal at the Parent Center is to provide our families with a center were they could feel inspired and confident to grow in. Our hope is to continue instilling growth, knowledge, and support for our community.

We look forward to working with you and providing you with support and assistance.   Our mission is promoting the student academic achievement through parent’s participation in their children’s education. The goal of the Parent Center is to be a place where parents can get involved in many ways; this goal can be reached through training, educating, and support parents as partners in their children’s education.   The Parent Center at Birmingham Community Charter High School is open every day from Monday through Friday, from 7:30 a.m to 4:00 p.m.


Objectives

  • To help parents develop parenting skills that will enable them to meet the basic obligations of family life and foster condition at home that emphasize the importance of education and learning.

  • Promoting clear, two-way communication between the school and families regarding school programs and students; progress.

  • Provide parents with the linkages and help them develop the skills they will need to access the school community support services to better strengthen student learning and development.


The following are some of the services that we provided to the parents:

  • Training for parents
  • Parental involvement
  • District policies: Parent Rights and Responsibilities
  • SSC/ELAC/CEAC
  • Absences / excuses and LAUSD complain procedure
  • Workshops for parents
  • Parent teacher conference
  • Parenting skills
  • Parent Institute classes
  • Contacting and referring parents to Kennedy HS resources
  • Assist teachers in the classroom
  • Translation for parents
