APEX ACADEMY

is a public charter school serving  7th - 12th grade students. As a public charter school, we are independent from the Los Angeles school district  allowing us to exercise our unique academic program designed to ensure our students obtain a quality education and the resources that match those of a private school. however what makes our school unique is our commitment to our students come first. Each student at APEX is part of our family: that means we are there to not only help them achieve excellence in their lives but we aim to nurture our relationship with the student and parents to ensure the social-emotion aspect for each student.

School History

APEX academy opened in September 2008 as an innovative competency-based school in LAUSD: a pilot program between LAUSD and Diploma Plus (supported by the  Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation). The financial crisis starting in 2008 forced APEX staff to become an independent public charter school to protect our innovative competency-based and social-emotional support model, plus much needed resources. Since our first graduating class in 2011, APEX academy has ensured that all alumni are fully supported in reaching their future goals, and have earned millions of dollars in financial aid through grants and scholarships: therefore, graduated from college with zero to limited debt.
