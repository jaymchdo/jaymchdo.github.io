We are APEX ACADEMY

Excellence is in our name, it is Academic Performance & Excellence Academy and we stand by it. Challenging our students to go aspire to college and chase challenging careers by engaging their individual passions. Matching each student with career explorations of his or her immediate interest. This is a departure from the more traditional academy or linked learning approach in which students are asked to choose from a small set of career themes.

we believe a turnaround occurs as the social-emotional needs of the student begin to be met  through counseling and provision  of wrap-around services, allowing  our students to connect with the  concept of family.

College & Career Readiness

Unlike most schools college readiness is more than just having students take A-G requirements alone. With us, it’s about ensuring each student has, thoroughly explored school and major options, access to resources like SAT/ACT prep and even help find money for college through grants in scholarships

Competency Based

APEX Academy is a competency-based school that has a rigorous curriculum, effective instructional practices, social/emotional support and a programming model designed to engage student learning based on each student’s individual academic needs, whether that is intervention or enrichment.
