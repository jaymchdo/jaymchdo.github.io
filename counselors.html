Counselors at APEX are the cornerstone for student success and well being. They know each student well and understand what it takes to help each of them achieve academically and personally. Counselors work closely with students and families to create school success plans for each student.

College & Career Readiness Through A-G

• The mission of LAUSD is to graduate students who are college-prepared and career-ready.

In the 21st century workplace, finding and keeping a job that pays well requires higher-order

thinking skills—those that we want every LAUSD student to learn.

• In order to accomplish this, students must take a rigorous high school program that teaches a

foundation of skills that will help them to apply, analyze and synthesize complex information.

• LAUSD has incorporated A-G courses into its graduation requirements prioritizing the courses

students need to ensure that they are college-prepared and career-ready by graduation day

for the Class of 2017 and beyond.

• By adopting A-G as the standard and requiring students pass with a ‘C,’ LAUSD is raising the

bar for all students to be competitive in today’s workforce, whether they go straight into a

career or go on to a 4-year college.

All research points to the importance of personalization and a low counselor to student ratio; at APEX Academy, you find research in practice. Our counselor to student has never been higher than 150 to 1 compared to the average of 400 to 1 and at times higher throughout the LA area. We believe that for our students to succeed, acces to quality counseling and guidance is a must.
